Superhero Characteristics Floral Exhibit

In this visualization, Superheros Characteristics are visualized in floral shapes.
Petal Shape and Color: Represents gender, with unique shapes and colors for each type.
Number of Petals: Indicates strength, with more petals denoting higher strength.
Petal Size: Corresponds to intelligence; larger petals represent higher intelligence.
Hovering on the flower: Display's Name, Alignment, Strength, Gender and Intelligence.
Clicking on the flower: Opens Link to superhero picture.

Visualization

Category: Combinatorial Visualization

This visualization is classified as "Combinatorial" due to its inspiration from Film Flowers by Shirley Wu. This approach presents data in a floral pattern, transcending standard chart types for a unique, visually engaging representation. I have adapted this approach to map superheros characteristics. The code references are Jesse Smith Byers blog for custom flower drawing and Annie Liao Wow's blog for drawing petal shapes.
